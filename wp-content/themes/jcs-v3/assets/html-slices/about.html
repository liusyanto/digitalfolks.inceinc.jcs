About Us

Dedication
backed by
Innovation

Serving customers for 20 years, Jurong Cold Store (JCS) is a fast-growing cold chain logistics and warehousing services company for the food & beverage industry. JCS operates a 2,000-pallet, fully-automated cold storage warehouse that offers a 24/7 efficient service solution.

Our proven track record in helping our customers manage their entire storage and distribution chain is rooted in our culture of innovation. We believe in continuously improving and expanding our capabilities by investing in cutting-edge technologies to offer superior performance in our operations.

Our practices are led by our customers’ needs, and our absolute commitment to service and quality is backed by the experience, dedication and professionalism of our people.

Our dependability is based on our personal touch and commitment to excellence. And this is why our customers trust us completely.
